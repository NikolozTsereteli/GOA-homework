Let's learn about animals



Hippo


Hippos are very massive animals, they are very small and cute when they are only babies, but they grow into massive beasts and cause countless fatalities, they grow up over thousand kilos and have a maximum bite force almost equal to that of an Aligator.




Black Panther


Black Panthers are extremely strong and durable and very agile species of the animal kingdom. They are a group of cat family alongside other species like the Tigers, although they are not as strong as Tigers or Lions, their speed and precision in hunting their pray makes them extremely danger for any human being on Earth.
