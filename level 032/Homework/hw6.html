The Transcendental Argument for God is the most powerful philosophical argument
- not only proving God's existence, but also affirming the Eastern Orthodox understanding of God as the fullness of truth.
It is such a strong argument because it is a defeater for other worldviews,
because they can not account for coherent epistemic justifications of Metaphysics,
nor the Transcnendental Categories. The Eastern Orthodox understanding
of the Monarchical Trinity's absolute Metaphysical Necessity is proven by the impossibility of the contrary.
